Blueberry Protein Smoothie

Cold, frothy smoothie that will replenish your body after an intense workout. Very easy to make and super delicious.

Ingredients

  • Protein powder
  • Leafy greens
  • Peanut butter
  • Frozen blueberries
  • Frozen banana
  • Almond milk
  • Ice or cold water

Directions

  1. In a blender add all the ingedients beginning with protein powder.
  2. Blend until it's at the consistency of your liking.
  3. Enjoy! Recipe usually yields two servings.
