Oatmeal

A nice serving of blueberry protein oatmeal.

A warm, hearty meal packed full of the essential macronutrients to regenerate you muscles after a long intense workout.

Ingredients

  • Oats
  • Water
  • Protein powder
  • Frozen blueberries
  • Pumpkin puree
  • Cinnamon
  • Banana
  • Peanut butter
  • Chocalate syrup

Directions

  1. In a large microwavable bowl add oats, blueberries, protein powder, cinnamon, and pumpkin puree.
  2. Slowly stir in water until the mixture becomes a slurry and at the consistency of your liking.
  3. Microwave for 5 minutes.
  4. Add peanut butter to hot bowl of oatmeal, slice bananas into bowl, and pour in chocalate syurup.
  5. Stir mixture and enjoy!
