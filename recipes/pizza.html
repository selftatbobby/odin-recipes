Pizza

Lavash bread pizza.

Delicious, low calorie pizza with any toppings you'd like.

Ingredients

  • Lavash bread
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fat free mozzerella
  • *Toppings

Directions

  1. On the lavash bread, spread a layer of tomato sauce.
  2. Sprinkle a layer of fat free mozzerella.
  3. Place desired *toppings on top of lavash bread.
  4. Add another layer of mozzerella.
  5. Bake for 450 degrees F, for 20 minutes or until cheese melted and toppings are cooked.
